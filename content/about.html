About MO

I love any excuse to be creative. I try to follow my highest joy and happiness each day as much as possible. In my whimsical free time, I oftentimes fantasize about living in my super-efficiently laid-out, gorgeous dream home; planning the next ultra-dream travel destination; or planning my next tea party, constantly coming up with any excuse to use my vintage tea paraphernalia.

In a previous life, I was a perennial administrative assistant across different industries; was oftentimes picked to be in the jury box; and I usually found myself volunteered to be on the company safety team.

Currently, aside from constantly learning and trying to keep up with the latest and greatest of tools to build beautiful, responsive websites alongside my wonderful husband, I lead a double-life as his Facebook secretary, nutritionist, dream analyzer, masseuse, card game opponent and muse, usually occurring during breaks in between working on websites. I aspire to play in a band, play the ukelele, and be a really super-badass code ninja. I'm an incessant list-taker, over-thinker, planner, and have been accused of Googling all the time.
